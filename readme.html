PetPal Tracker - Read Me

Privacy Policy

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it.

How to Use the App

1. Select your pets. 2. View care info. 3. Use the 7-day tracker.

Safety Issues

As data is stored locally, data loss may occur if device storage is cleared. We are not responsible for any lost data.

AdSense Policy

Ads are shown when online to support the app. Please refer to Google AdSense policies for details.

More Information

This app is designed to help you track the care of aquatic pets. It is a Progressive Web App (PWA) for offline access.
